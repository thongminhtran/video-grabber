# Video Grabber

A browser extension that lets you download videos playing on any website.

Works on Edge, Chrome, Brave.

## How to install

### Step 1 — Download

Click the green **Code** button at the top right of this page → **Download ZIP**.
Unzip the folder anywhere (Desktop is fine).

### Step 2 — Load the extension

1. Open `edge://extensions/` (or `chrome://extensions/`)
2. Turn on **Developer mode** (top right)
3. Click **Load unpacked**
4. Pick the `dist` folder inside the unzipped folder
5. Pin the new icon to your toolbar

Done. You can now download videos from most websites.

### Step 3 — *(optional)* enable YouTube, Twitter, TikTok, etc.

Double-click **`Setup.cmd`** in the unzipped folder. Wait a minute. That's it.

## How to use

1. Open a website with a video playing
2. Click the **Video Grabber icon** in your toolbar
3. Pick the quality you want
4. Click **Download**
5. Choose where to save it

## Removing it

Double-click **`Uninstall.cmd`** to remove the background helper, then go to `edge://extensions/` and click **Remove** on the extension.
